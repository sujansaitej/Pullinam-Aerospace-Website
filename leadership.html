Leadership

C.S. Karunakaran – Founder & CEO

C.S. Karunakaran brings 15 years of experience in light aircraft maintenance and drone technology. He is the inventor of Asia's First Flying Bike and played a crucial role in drone operations during the COVID-19 pandemic. His visionary leadership is driving Pullinam’s mission to revolutionize ultralight aircraft manufacturing in India.

S.B. Shiva Prasaath, an AI expert, co-founded PULLINAM Aerospace Technologies with the mission to integrate cutting-edge technology into aviation. He oversees strategic development, operations, and technological innovation. With a portfolio of research in AI, machine learning, and embedded systems, he contributes to the advancement of predictive maintenance, automation, and smart aviation technologies.
