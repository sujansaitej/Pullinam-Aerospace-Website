AeroCon 2024

Our founder (C S Karunakaran) was part of SAEINDIA AeroCon 2024 as organizing committee, which took place on 6th and 7th of June 2024. This is a leading aerospace and defense conference and exhibition that took place in Bangalore. The event gathered professionals, engineers, innovators, and industry leaders from around the world to explore the latest trends, technologies, and innovations in the aerospace and aviation sectors.

All India Seminar on Trends in Aviation

All India Seminar on Trends in Aviation (Civil and Defense) was held on 30th and 31st of August 2024 under the aegis of Aerospace Engineering Division board, IE(I) in association with IAAA (Institution of Aeronautics, Astronautics, and Aviation). The event was partnered by Pullinam Aerospace Technologies at Periyar Maniammai Institute of Science, Thanjavur.

Design Launch PAT-LSA1

We proudly launched the design of our first microlight aircraft, the PAT-LSA1, at Startup Thiruvizhla, Madurai. This milestone represents a significant step forward in our commitment to sustainable and innovative aviation. The PAT-LSA1 is designed to meet the evolving demands of the aviation industry while prioritizing efficiency and eco-friendliness. We are excited about the potential of this aircraft to revolutionize microlight aviation and contribute to a greener future. Thank you to everyone who has supported us on this journey!

Setting Up Aircraft Design Centre at Hyderabad

Pullinam Aerospace Technologies, in collaboration with MLRIT Hyderabad, has launched an Aircraft Design Centre on the MLRIT campus. This facility will support both ongoing and future design initiatives of Pullinam Aerospace Technologies. The center aims to engage over 200 students and faculty from academia, working alongside industry experts in various aircraft design projects.
