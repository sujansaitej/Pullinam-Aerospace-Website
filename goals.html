Our Goals

At Pullinam Aerospace, we are driven by both short-term and long-term ambitions aimed at reshaping the future of sustainable aviation and aerospace technologies.

Short-term Goals

  • Launch the PAT-LSA1 within the next 18 months.
  • Establish flying clubs in 100 engineering universities and autonomous colleges across India within 3-6 years.
  • Expand into key regional markets, focusing on South Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

Long-term Goals

  • Achieve a revenue milestone of INR 10,000 Crore within 10 years of launching our first aircraft.
  • Become a global leader in sustainable aircraft manufacturing, focusing on the development of eco-friendly aviation technologies.
