Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable future in aviation by offering high-performance, eco-friendly solutions that meet the global emissions challenge

Who are we ?

Pullinam Aerospace Technologies Private Limited is an Indian startup with a bold mission: to lead the light aircraft manufacturing sector and reduce aviation’s carbon footprint. We aim to develop innovative, pilotless, and environmentally sustainable aircraft

Vision

We are driven by a vision to tackle the global emissions challenge using a variety of aircraft designs and propulsion systems, including electric propulsion, SAF, and other non-electric solutions.

Pullinam

The name “Pullinam” comes from an ancient Tamil word meaning a species of bird, found in Tamil literary classics such as Silappathigaram, Seevaga Sinthamani, and Kalithogai. This reflects our commitment to lightweight, agile, and eco-conscious designs inspired by nature.

Uniqueness

We are dedicated to reducing carbon emissions through the development of electric propulsion and the use of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) in our aircraft.

FOUNDER & CEO

C.S.Karunakaran

C.S. Karunakaran brings 15 years of experience in light aircraft maintenance and drone technology. He is the inventor of Asia's First Flying Bike and played a crucial role in drone operations during the COVID-19 pandemic, supporting the Chennai City Police. His visionary leadership is driving Pullinam’s mission to revolutionize ultralight aircraft manufacturing in India.

CO-FOUNDER & CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

S.B. Shiva Prasaath

S.B. Shiva Prasaath is an accomplished AI expert and visionary, co-founding PULLINAM Aerospace Technologies with a mission to integrate cutting-edge technology into the aviation industry. As Chief Operating Officer, Shiva Prasaath oversees the strategic development, operational management, and technological innovation within the company. Beyond his leadership role, Shiva Prasaath has an impressive portfolio of research publications in the fields of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and embedded systems. His work has contributed to advancements in predictive maintenance systems, automation, and smart applications across industries. His research is highly regarded in both academic and professional circles, and it demonstrates his commitment to leveraging AI for real-world impact.
