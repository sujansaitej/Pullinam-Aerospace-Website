Our Aircraft

Our product line is designed to meet diverse aviation needs, from recreational microlight aircraft to advanced electric and SAF-powered vehicles for business and regional transport. Products in development include:

  • 2-seater Microlight Aircraft
  • 2-seater Light-Sport Aircraft (LSA)
  • 2-seater STOL and CTOL Aircraft (electric and non-electric options)
  • 4-6 seater Aircraft powered by SAF or electric propulsion systems
  • 4-seater VTOL Aircraft
  • 10-seater SAF-powered Aircraft

Our first product, the PAT-LSA1, is a lightweight, high-performance electric and non-electric Light Sport Aircraft (LSA), which will set the foundation for our upcoming innovations in both electric propulsion and sustainable aviation.
