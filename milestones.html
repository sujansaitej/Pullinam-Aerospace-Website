Our Milestones

2023

Company Establishment: Pullinam Aerospace was incorporated on June 23rd, 2023, marking the beginning of their aerospace innovation journey.

2024

Partnership with MLRIT: In October 2024, Pullinam Aerospace collaborated with MLRIT to develop a 10-seater turboprop aircraft.

2024

Leadership and Vision: The company is led by Selvaraj Karunakaran and Sudha Balamurugan, pioneering their entry into aerospace technology.

2024

Industry Collaborations: Pullinam initiated talks with major aerospace companies for collaborative research at AeroCON 2024.

2024

AeroCON 2024 Participation: Pullinam Aerospace is preparing to showcase their innovations at AeroCON 2024 in Bengaluru.

2024

Strategic Growth: The company is exploring more collaborations in unmanned aerial systems and regional aircraft design.
