Pullinam Aerospace Sponsoring Aerothon 2024 Phase 2

Pullinam Aerospace is proud to be a sponsor of Aerothon 2024 Phase 2! ✈

Aerothon is an innovative competition that brings together bright minds from around the globe to tackle real-world challenges in the aerospace sector. Participants will collaborate, brainstorm, and develop cutting-edge solutions that could shape the future of aviation and space exploration.

As an emerging startup in aerospace technology, Pullinam Aerospace is committed to fostering creativity and driving progress in our industry. We believe that by supporting initiatives like Aerothon, we can inspire the next generation of innovators and contribute to advancements that will propel us into the future.

Join us on this exciting journey as we push the boundaries of what’s possible in aerospace!
